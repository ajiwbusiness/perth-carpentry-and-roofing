finished the moment it's deployed — no placeholder art. Every photo below is from **Pexels**
or **Unsplash**, both of which grant free commercial use with **no attribution required**
(Pexels License / Unsplash License). Attribution is listed here anyway as good practice and
so each source can be re-verified.

> These are **demo/stock photos**. For a real business, swap them for the company's own
> project photos before or right after the sale — a homeowner seeing actual finished roofs
> converts far better than stock. See `CUSTOMIZE.md §4` for the file-by-file swap guide.

## Notes for a real deployment
- The **before/after pair** are two different tile roofs chosen to read as one restoration.
  For a live business, replace with a genuine before/after of the **same home** — it's the
  most persuasive proof on the page.
- The stock roofing pool skews toward tile; the gallery/hero were biased toward US-style
  suburban asphalt-shingle homes. A real business's own asphalt work will look even more local.
- All photos are optimized WebP (hero ~185 KB, gallery tiles 20–160 KB) for fast mobile load.
  If you re-export replacements, target ~1000×750 for gallery tiles, 1200×800 for the
  before/after pair (identical dimensions so the slider aligns), 1000×1250 for the About
  portrait, and ~1920×1080 for the hero.
