Good to know

Roofing questions, answered

How much does a new roof cost?

It depends on the size, pitch, and material of your roof. Most residential replacements fall in a typical range, and you'll get an exact, itemized price in writing after your free inspection — before you commit to anything. Financing is available to spread the cost into monthly payments.

Do you help with insurance claims?

Yes. For storm and hail damage we inspect your roof for free, document everything with photos, provide detailed estimates, and meet your adjuster on-site so nothing legitimate is missed. We guide you through the process — though the claim decision and your deductible are always set by your insurer, not by us.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roofs are torn off and fully replaced in one to two days, weather permitting. Larger or more complex roofs can take longer. We'll give you a clear timeline in your estimate and keep you updated throughout.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — Perth Carpentry and Roofing is fully licensed, bonded, and insured, and our installers are manufacturer-certified. That protects you, your home, and your material warranty. We're happy to provide our license and insurance details on request.

What warranty do you offer?

Your roof is covered two ways: the manufacturer warranty on the materials, and our 10-year workmanship warranty on the installation. If a covered issue comes up, we come back and make it right.

Is the inspection really free?

Completely free, with no obligation. We'll inspect your roof, show you photos of what we find, and give you a straight recommendation — repair, replace, or you're fine for now. No scare tactics, no pressure to buy.
